Dr. Amrendra Kumar

MBBS,MD, DM,

Dr. Amrendra Kumar is the Co-founder & Director of DermaClinix and is
amongst the best dermatologists in South Delhi and hair transplant surgeon
in Delhi, INDIA. He has done his MD in Dermatology from the well-known All
India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), DELHI. He also has worked as a
senior resident in the prestigious Post Graduate Institute for Medical Education
and Research (PGIMER), Chandigarh. After that, he joined one of the largest
hospitals in Delhi- Safdarjung Hospital. Working in these apex institutes has
provided him vast experience and knowledge in every field of dermatology.
Whether it is hair transplantation or cosmetic surgeries or clinical dermatology
, Dr. Amrendra has proven himself as a hair and skin expert.
