DR V K Gupta

MBBS,MD, DM,

Dr. (Col.) V K Gupta is a renowned Gastroenterologist in Pitampura, North Delhi.
He has been practicing for more than three decades now and has established a positive
image in the minds of patients. His MBBS, Masters and DM degrees all have been pursued
from prestigious Indian medical colleges. Since he has been practice for a long time,
he has developed an expertise in hepatology, all endoscopic procedures, treatment of
liver diseases and many more.
